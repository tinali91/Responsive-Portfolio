About Me

My name is Tina and I currently work in marketing and communications at the University of Colorado Boulder, where I graduated in 2014. I graduated with a dual degree in Integrative Physiology and Psychology and originally started college with the intention of going to medical school. Needless to say, that didn't happen and now here I am in a coding class learning how to make things work on the interwebs.


In my free time, I like to spend time with my dog, Winston, who is adorable. I also volunteer a lot with various nonprofit organizations including Heritage Camps for Adoptive Families (HCAF) and the Union of North American Vietnamese Student Associations (UNAVSA).
